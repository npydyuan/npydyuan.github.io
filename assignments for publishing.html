assignments for publishing

Here are some assignments you could give yourself for a sojourn into the world of publishing:

  • a back-and-forth poem cycle for an agreed upon week (or weeks) of the semester for anyone who wants to join. The poem grows as it passes from person to person. You have to take your turn within a certain period of time or it passes to the next person (no hard feelings).

  • a "type-in" day where participants commit to typing (or writing) all day, and as the day progresses, everyone uploads their pages or fragments to a Padlet or shared doc of some kind.

  • Submit to Phoenix Review — that document may be outdated, but ask me and I can find out about any upcoming deadlines.

  • Submit writing to other contest(s), magazine(s), web publication(s), or scholarship(s), and report back on the experience.

  • MAKE AN ANTHOLOGY---If anyone wants to take responsibility for gathering contributions from other people in this class and editing them together into some kind of collection (whether it be a print-on-demand book, website, or ...?), let me know. I can help but you would be ultimately in charge of seeing it through. This could be a group project.

  • Blogging, posting, social media (ugh), or whatever---share your work with the online community of your choice and give us the link and a little reflection/statement/etc.

  • Produce a play (recruit friends/family as actors?) and share in person or online.

  • Make some other kind of video or audio of your work and share it.
