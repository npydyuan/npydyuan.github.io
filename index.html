Welcome!

As my favorite Aranea Cavatica once said, “Greetings and salutations!”

This little website is all about the practice of writing.

We acknowledge and are guided by the usual learning targets and Wisconsin English-Language Arts Literacy Standards, if you would like to refer to those.

But the main thing I want you to do is to write, preferably every day, and find out what you can learn by being persistent and consistent with that.

You are invited to choose your own path.

I would also like you to participate in a series of revision projects.
