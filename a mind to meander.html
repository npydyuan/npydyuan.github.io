a mind to meander

Did you know the word meander comes from “a river in Phrygia, proverbial for its many windings”? That seems appropriate. The idea behind this part of the site is that you can meander along, upstream or downstream or branching off to follow a tributary or distributary.

Whenever you want to stop and write, stop and write. Then you can either be finished for the time being, or else come back in and wander some more.
